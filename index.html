  3. 2008 Lady Gaga's debut album, The Fame is released to rave reviews, but no song garners more attention than "Paparazzi."
  4. 2009 Gaga comes back stronger with her second album The Fame Monster only a year later. Hits such as "Bad Romance" and "Alejandro" dominate the airwaves.
  5. 2011 Gaga's third album, Born This Way, releases to massive success and,for many fans and critics, remains her best album.
  6. 2013 ARTPOP releases and some see this album as the beginning of the fall. Songs from this album are deemed "overwhelming" and "excessive"
  7. 2014 Lady Gaga and Tony Bennett produce a jazz album together entitled Cheek to Cheek. Even though it is not relatively well-known to fans, it establishes Gaga as a multifaceted artist
  8. 2016 Gaga returns to form with her self-titled album, Joanne. The album is soulful and country music-inspired, including ballad "A Million Reasons."
  9. 2017 Gaga headlines music festival Coachella, the first woman to do so in over a decade. Playing it rather safe, she debuts new song, "The Cure," which has a conventional-sounding pop vibe instead of Gaga's usual
  10. 2018 Gaga stars in the third remake of 1937 film A Star Is Born with Bradley Cooper. The story follows a young woman who falls in love with a singer and becomes a starlet.
