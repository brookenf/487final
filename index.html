A Short History →

  1. 2008 Lady Gaga becomes an overnight worldwide sensation with her song "Just Dance."
  2. 2008 Lady Gaga's debut album, The Fame is released to rave reviews, including hit "Paparazzi."
  3. 2009 Gaga releases The Fame Monster as a deluxe edition of The Fame. Hits such as "Bad Romance" dominate the airwaves.
  4. 2011 Gaga's second studio album, Born This Way, releases and, for many fans and critics, remains her best album.
  5. 2013 ARTPOP releases to less warm recepetion. Songs from this album are deemed "overwhelming" and "excessive."
  6. 2014 Lady Gaga and Tony Bennett produce a jazz album together entitled Cheek to Cheek. The album establishes Gaga as a multi-faceted artist.
  7. 2016 Gaga releases Joanne, named after an aunt. The album is country music-inspired, including ballad "A Million Reasons."
  8. 2017 Gaga headlines music festival Coachella, the first woman to do so in over a decade. She debuts "The Cure," which has a conventional-sounding pop vibe.
  9. 2017 Gaga performs at the Super Bowl and comes in hot by soaring in from the sky.
  10. 2018 Gaga stars in the third remake of 1937 film A Star Is Born with Bradley Cooper. The story follows a young woman who falls in love with a singer and becomes a starlet.
